# Earnings Analysis Playbook

Follow these steps to produce a thorough earnings analysis:

## 1. Revenue Assessment
- Compare reported revenue to analyst consensus estimates
- Calculate year-over-year and quarter-over-quarter growth rates
- Identify revenue by segment if available — flag which segments are accelerating or decelerating

## 2. Profitability Metrics
- Gross margin trend (expanding or compressing?)
- Operating margin vs. prior quarter and prior year
- Net income and EPS vs. consensus — note the magnitude of any beat or miss

## 3. Cash Flow and Balance Sheet
- Free cash flow generation — is the company self-funding?
- Debt-to-equity ratio and any recent changes
- Cash position and runway

## 4. Forward Guidance
- Compare management guidance to current analyst estimates
- Note any changes in full-year outlook (raised, maintained, or lowered)
- Identify key risks or catalysts mentioned in the earnings call

## 5. Market Reaction Context
- Check post-earnings price movement
- Compare current valuation (P/E, P/S) to historical range
- Assess whether the market reaction is justified by the data

## 6. Recommendation Framework
- **Buy signal**: Revenue beat + raised guidance + margin expansion
- **Hold signal**: Mixed results, in-line with expectations
- **Sell signal**: Revenue miss + lowered guidance + margin compression
- Always caveat with sector context and macro environment
