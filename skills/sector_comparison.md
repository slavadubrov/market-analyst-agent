# Sector Comparison Framework

Follow these steps to compare a stock against its sector peers:

## 1. Identify Peer Group
- Find 3-5 direct competitors in the same sector and market cap range
- Use search_competitors tool to get relative metrics
- Note any differences in business model that affect comparability

## 2. Valuation Comparison
- Compare P/E ratios across the peer group
- Compare P/S (Price-to-Sales) ratios — especially for growth companies
- Calculate EV/EBITDA if data is available
- Rank the target stock: premium, in-line, or discount to peers

## 3. Growth Comparison
- Revenue growth rate (YoY) vs. peer median
- Earnings growth rate vs. peer median
- Is the target growing faster or slower than its valuation implies?

## 4. Profitability Comparison
- Gross margin vs. peer group
- Operating margin vs. peer group
- Return on equity (ROE) if available

## 5. Relative Value Assessment
- PEG ratio (P/E divided by earnings growth rate) — lower is cheaper
- If target trades at a premium: is the growth differential sufficient to justify it?
- If target trades at a discount: is there a fundamental reason (risk, declining growth)?

## 6. Output Format
Present findings as a comparison table with the target stock highlighted, followed by a 2-3 sentence relative value conclusion.
